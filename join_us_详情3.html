Research Scientist Internship (PhD Students)

Position Information

  • Position: Research Scientist Intern (PhD Students)
  • Number of Positions: Multiple
  • Research Areas: Medical Image Processing, Medical Information Science, Embodied Intelligence, Computer Vision, Large Model Attack & Defense, Neural Radiance Fields, Large Model Fingerprinting, Model Protocol Security, etc.

Job Description

  • Attend weekly group meetings, report research progress and submit weekly reports.
  • Complete assigned research tasks, including paper reviews and related work.
  • Write research papers and submit to high-level conferences or journals.

Requirements

  • Academic Background: Computer Science and Technology, Software Engineering, or related fields.
  • Technical Skills: Familiar with machine learning frameworks such as PyTorch.
  • Research Enthusiasm: Strong interest in research and willingness to invest time.
  • Education: Current PhD students, internship duration of at least 6 months.
  • Research Areas (meet any one):
    • Large Model Attack & Defense: Understanding of large model jailbreaking and defense.
    • Neural Radiance Fields: Ability to read and understand Neural Radiance Fields related code.
    • Large Model Fingerprinting: Experience in large model fine-tuning.
    • Embodied Intelligence: Experience in adversarial attacks, model backdoors, vision large models, or large language models.
    • Model Protocol Security: Familiar with security analysis and protection mechanisms of large model communication protocols.
    • Medical Information & Image Processing: Experience in medical image processing (segmentation, classification, reconstruction) or medical information analysis (electronic health records, privacy protection).

Benefits & Development

  • Internship Certificate: Internship certificate and recommendation letter provided for internships over 6 months.
  • Growth Opportunities: Collaborate with top teams, explore cutting-edge fields; outstanding candidates will be prioritized for advanced studies at top institutions and assisted in publishing CCF-A/B or high-level SCI papers.
  • Performance Rewards: Rewards based on contributions.
  • Other Benefits: Flexible online internship, excellent research guidance and international exchange opportunities.

We look forward to your participation in exploring the infinite possibilities of research! For any questions, please feel free to contact us.
