浙江大学滨江研究院博士后研究员招聘公告

浙江大学滨江研究院面向海内外优秀青年学者招聘博士后研究员，致力于推动计算机科学领域的创新研究。我们欢迎具有扎实学术基础和创新潜力的优秀人才加入，共同探索前沿技术。招聘详情如下：

招聘岗位

  • 岗位名称：博士后研究员
  • 招聘人数：若干名
  • 研究方向：计算机视觉、深度学习、多模态大模型、自然语言处理、医疗等相关领域。

应聘条件

  • 具有博士学位，或已提交博士论文答辩（预计近期获得博士学位）。
  • 在相关领域至少有一篇一区（Q1）期刊或会议论文发表记录。
  • 具有强烈的科研兴趣和良好的团队合作精神，英语水平良好。
  • 博士毕业3年内，年龄一般不超过35周岁，身体健康。

工作职责与出站要求

  • 参与学院重点研究项目，进行独立或合作科研工作。
  • 出站要求：在站期间发表2-3篇高水平学术论文，或获得专利授权，或主持/参与一项国家级/省级基金项目。

待遇与福利

  • 年薪：待遇丰厚，年薪30-50万元，具体面议（根据个人能力和经验，提供具有竞争力的薪酬包，包括基本工资、绩效奖金）。
  • 住房与补贴：杭州政府补贴：符合杭州市人才政策可申请100万元购房补贴；E类人才可获政府人才保障。
  • 其他福利：享受浙江大学博士后相关待遇，包括医疗保险、社会保险、住房公积金，子女教育支持，提供良好的科研环境，实验平台和国际交流机会。

申请方式

  • 请将个人简历（包括教育背景、研究经历、发表论文列表等）、代表性学术成果（PDF格式）发送至招聘邮箱：recruit@zju-if.com。
  • 邮件主题：博士后申请-姓名-研究方向。
  • 申请截止日期：滚动招聘，常年有效，直至岗位满额。

浙江大学期待您的加入，一起共创计算机科学的美好未来！如有疑问，欢迎随时咨询。

Postdoctoral Researcher Position at Zhejiang University Binjiang Institute

Zhejiang University Binjiang Institute is recruiting outstanding postdoctoral researchers from home and abroad to advance innovative research in computer science. We welcome talented individuals with solid academic foundations and innovative potential to join us in exploring cutting-edge technologies. Details are as follows:

Position Information

  • Position: Postdoctoral Researcher
  • Number of Positions: Multiple
  • Research Areas: Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Multimodal Large Models, Natural Language Processing, Medical Applications, and related fields.

Requirements

  • Hold a Ph.D. degree or have submitted Ph.D. dissertation for defense (expected to obtain degree soon).
  • Have at least one Q1 journal or conference publication in relevant fields.
  • Demonstrate strong research interest and excellent teamwork skills, with good English proficiency.
  • Within 3 years of Ph.D. graduation, generally under 35 years old, in good health.

Responsibilities & Exit Requirements

  • Participate in key institute research projects, conduct independent or collaborative research.
  • Exit Requirements: Publish 2-3 high-quality academic papers during the position, or obtain patent authorization, or lead/participate in national/provincial funding projects.

Compensation & Benefits

  • Annual Salary: Competitive package of 300,000-500,000 RMB annually (negotiable based on qualifications and experience, including base salary and performance bonuses).
  • Housing & Subsidies: Hangzhou government subsidies: Eligible for up to 1,000,000 RMB housing subsidy under Hangzhou talent policy; Category E talent benefits available.
  • Other Benefits: Full Zhejiang University postdoc benefits including medical insurance, social insurance, housing fund, education support for children, excellent research environment, experimental platforms, and international exchange opportunities.

Application Process

  • Please send your CV (including educational background, research experience, publication list), representative academic achievements (PDF format) to: recruit@zju-if.com.
  • Email Subject: Postdoc Application - Name - Research Area.
  • Application Deadline: Rolling recruitment, open year-round until positions are filled.

Zhejiang University looks forward to your participation in creating a bright future for computer science! For any questions, please feel free to contact us.
