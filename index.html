Gentel Research Institute

Zhejiang University Binjiang Research Institute gathers global talents, focusing on forward-looking applied research and achievement transformation in key fields like digital economy and intelligent manufacturing. It links the university's academics with Binjiang's industrial needs to build a high-level industry-academia platform for regional tech and industrial development.

RESEARCH SPOTLIGHTS

Text Watermarking and Model Fingerprint
Developing advanced techniques for embedding invisible watermarks in text and creating unique model fingerprints to protect intellectual property and ensure model authenticity in AI systems.
Reinforcement Learning
Exploring cutting-edge reinforcement learning algorithms and applications, focusing on multi-agent systems, policy optimization, and real-world deployment challenges.
Agentic Systems
Building intelligent autonomous agents that can reason, plan, and interact in complex environments, with applications in robotics, virtual assistants, and decision-making systems.
